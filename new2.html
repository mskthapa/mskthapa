Beluga

Description

Beluga lives at #123 Freddy Fazbear Pizza in Honolulu, Hawaii.

He is most likely to be 19 years old, but is shown in different grades depending on the video.

His bedtime is always at 7pm, even though his school days are always random, and always start at random times of day.

While he is human, he turns into a polite cat in the middle of the night.
